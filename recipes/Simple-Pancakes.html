Simple Pancakes

Description

ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour in some batter and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook the other side, then serve.
