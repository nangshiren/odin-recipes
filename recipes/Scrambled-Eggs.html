Scrambled Eggs

Description:

These are delicious and easy-to-make scrambled eggs that are perfect for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter. Allow it to melt and coat the pan.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it cook undisturbed for a few seconds until it starts to set around the edges.
  4. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs from the edges towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Continue cooking and stirring until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny. Remove from heat as they will continue to cook with residual heat.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your scrambled eggs!
