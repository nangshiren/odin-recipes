Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

This is a classic grilled cheese sandwich recipe that's simple to make and always satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the cheese between the slices of bread, buttered sides facing each other.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place the sandwich in the skillet.
  4. Cook for a few minutes until the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your grilled cheese sandwich!
